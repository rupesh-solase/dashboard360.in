Common Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Absolutely. Your data never leaves your own systems without your permission. We use Microsoft Power BI — which is enterprise-grade software trusted by Fortune 500 companies worldwide. All data connections are secured with encryption. We do not store your raw business data on our servers. Your Tally or Excel data stays on your computer — we only connect to read it and display it on your dashboard. You have full control at all times.

No technical team needed at all! That is the whole point of Dashboard360. Your accountant continues working in Tally or Excel exactly as before — nothing changes for them. We handle all the technical setup, data connection and dashboard building on our side. Once your dashboard is ready, we give you a 30-minute training session in your language (Hindi, Marathi or English). After that, you simply open the dashboard on your phone or laptop every morning — just like opening WhatsApp. No IT knowledge required!

For most small businesses — No! Power BI Desktop is completely free to use on your computer. If you want to view the dashboard on your phone or share it with multiple team members, you may need Power BI Pro (approximately ₹700–800 per user per month). However, for single-owner businesses, we can set up a free sharing method so you can view everything without any extra license cost. We will guide you on the most cost-effective option based on your specific needs during our free consultation call.

Typically 5 to 7 working days from the day we receive your data. Simple dashboards for single companies can be ready in 3 days. Complex multi-company dashboards with Tally integration may take up to 10 days. We keep you updated at every step and share a preview before final delivery.

That is exactly what we are built for! We set up automatic data refresh so your dashboard updates every morning without any manual work. Your accountant exports from Tally or saves the Excel file — and the dashboard refreshes automatically. You see fresh numbers every day when you wake up — just like your WhatsApp messages!
